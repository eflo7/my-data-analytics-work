\### 1. \[Spatial Analysis: Obesity Rates vs. Restaurant Density](./Project\_01\_Alteryx\_Health\_Data)

\* \*\*Objective:\*\* Determine if there is a geographic link between obesity rates and the concentration of "health-focused" vs. general restaurants in US cities.

\* \*\*What I Did:\*\*

&nbsp;   \* Built an \*\*Alteryx workflow\*\* to blend CDC health data with Yelp business listings and US Zip Codes.

&nbsp;   \* Used \*\*Regex\*\* to parse messy text fields into usable Latitude/Longitude coordinates.

&nbsp;   \* Performed \*\*Spatial Matching\*\* to visualize commercial density against health outcomes.
